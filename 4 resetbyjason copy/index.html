About

One specialist. Start to finish.

I'm Jason. I run Reset by Jason a little less like a car wash and a little more like a studio appointment — one client, one car, the time it actually needs. When you book with me, you get me. Not a rotating crew. Not an assembly line.

I came to detailing the same way I came to organizing overwhelmed spaces: a deep dislike of "good enough." The wheels get the time they need. The gum comes out of the carpet. The walker path gets measured.

If you've been putting off the inside of your car, or the garage you can't park in, or the patio you stopped using — that's exactly the kind of work I do.

What you can expect

  • Honest scoping. If it needs four hours, I'll tell you. If it needs one, I'll tell you that too.
  • Transparent pricing. pricing of $65/hr with a 2-hour minimum.
  • No rush. One car at a time. One client at a time.
  • Care for your stuff. Nothing gets dragged, dropped, or scratched.
